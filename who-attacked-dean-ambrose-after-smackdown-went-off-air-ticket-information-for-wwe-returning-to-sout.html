Who Attacked Dean Ambrose?

After WWE Smackdown ended in Milwaukee, Wisconsin last night, The Wyatt Family came out and attacked Dean Ambrose. 

John Cena rushed down the ramp and made the save. Ambrose and Cena hit their finishers and cleared the ring of the family to end the show. 

WWE Returns To Greenville

WWE Raw will return to the Bon Secours Wellness Arena in Greenville, South Carolina on December 8th, 2014. Tickets for the even will go on sale on October 3rd. Advertised for the show includes John Cena, Seth Rollins, Dean Ambrose, Bray Wyatt, Kane and more.
