New Matches Added to 12/4 WWE RAW Episode

WWE’s Adam Pearce just dropped some major announcements for the December 4 episode of Monday Night Raw! Brace yourselves for an action-packed night with two thrilling tag team clashes.

First up, former NXT Women’s Tag Team Champs Kayden Carter & Katana Chance will face off against the formidable duo of Natalya and Tegan Nox. Expect sparks to fly as these talented teams collide in the squared circle!

And that’s not all – Julius & Brutus Creed, fresh off their impressive victory, are set to battle it out with Dominik Mysterio and JD McDonagh of Judgment Day. The Creeds are on a mission to prove their mettle and earn a shot at the Undisputed WWE Tag Team Championship currently held by Finn Balor and Damian Priest.

Here’s a sneak peek at full card for WWE RAW 12/4:

  • WWE World Heavyweight Championship: Seth Rollins (c) vs. Jey Uso
  • Drew McIntyre vs. Sami Zayn
  • Nia Jax vs. Shayna Baszler
  • The Creed Brothers (Julius & Brutus Creed) vs. Judgment Day (Dominik Mysterio & JD McDonagh)
  • Natalya & Tegan Nox vs. Katana Chance & Kayden Carter
  • Two-Out-Of-Three Falls: DIY (Tommaso Ciampa & Johnny Gargano) vs. Imperium (Giovanni Vince & Ludwig Kaiser)
  • Cody Rhodes addresses Shinsuke Nakamura

Get ready for a night of electrifying action and intense rivalries! Tune in to WWE Raw on December 4 at 8 p.m. ET, and stay tuned to Ringside News for live coverage. Don’t miss a moment of the action!
