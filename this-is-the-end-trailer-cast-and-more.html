Synopsis

Frequent collaborators James Franco and Seth Rogen are reuniting for This is the End. The actors play themselves in this meta-comedy that costars Jay Baruchel, Danny McBride, Jonah Hill and Craig Robinson. It’s the end of days and the men are trapped in an apartment as the apocalypse destroys Los Angeles. Expect lots of cameos from the likes of Emma Watson, Jason Segel, Mindy Kaling and Rihanna. This is the End also marks the directorial debut of Rogen alongside Evan Goldberg.
