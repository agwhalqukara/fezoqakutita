Is oven-safe the same as broiler-safe in the same way?

Both yes and no. Some types of dishes, such as Pyrex, are oven-safe but should not be used under the broiler, despite the fact that they are oven-safe. Thermal shock (very rapid temperature fluctuations) may cause Pyrex to fracture or shatter, despite the fact that the material is oven-safe.
